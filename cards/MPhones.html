The Mobile Revolution

2000s - Present

The computational power of historical room-sized supercomputers now completely fits directly into your pocket. Modern smartphones and tablets have completely rewritten the rules of communication, remote workspace, and interactive entertainment media.

With high-speed cellular infrastructure and continuous cloud connectivity, mobile units serve as primary tools driving our daily routine operations today.

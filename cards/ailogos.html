Generative AI

2020s

The **2020s** marked the rapid global emergence of Large Language Models (LLMs), such as ChatGPT. Artificial Intelligence became capable of autonomously writing clean code architectures, generating artworks, and sustaining deep contextual arguments.

This milestone shift changed creative industries, accelerating production speed and pushing computational interaction toward a human-like boundary.
